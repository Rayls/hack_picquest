Welcome to Pigio.

Pigio is a photography game that makes exploring Halifax fun as a newcomer or a long-time resident. Take pictures, get achievements, race against your friends, and complete quests!

Facebook Integration

Pigio makes use of Facebook's API to deliver a unique experience whereby users can seamlessly share there progress on their preexisting social networks.
